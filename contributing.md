### What is Tomoe?

The name "Tomoe" (巴) comes from a traditional Japanese comma-shaped swirl symbol representing harmony, motion, and unity . It was also the name of Tomoe Gakuen, a progressive school in Tokyo founded by educator Sosaku Kobayashi, where children learned through freedom, creativity, and individualized attention . The school's emblem was two tomoe — one black and one white — forming a perfect circle, symbolizing the balanced development of body and mind .

This software carries forward that philosophy: learning should be natural, joyful, and tailored to each individual's pace.
